About Sun Hearing Aid

Delivering patient‑focused audiology, modern hearing devices and lasting aftercare.

Our Mission

To restore clear communication and improve quality of life through evidence‑based hearing care and personalised service.

We combine clinical expertise with the latest hearing technology to provide assessments, tailored fittings and long‑term support.

Our Approach

Holistic, patient‑centred and outcome driven.

  • Detailed diagnostic testing
  • Individualised device selection
  • Personal fittings, tuning and counselling
  • Follow‑up care, repairs and education

Clinic & Equipment

Our clinic is equipped for full audiometric testing, speech‑in‑noise assessment and real‑ear measurement. We partner with leading manufacturers to offer a broad range of devices.
